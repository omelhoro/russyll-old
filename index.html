As you may see this app is not only about dividing syllables but about reflecting phonetic forms of russian. Mainly: A word is first edited orphoepically: Hard-to-pronounce clusters like "чувство,сердце" turn to pronouncable units -> "ств,рц". Then some phonetic rules are applied like assimilation "мужской -> мушской". You'll notice that the algorhithm converts palatalized consonants into uppercase. The reason is that it matters to the syllable model 4 whether the consonant is palatalized or velarized. Follow the development here.
